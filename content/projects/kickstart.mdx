#### OVERVIEW

# The best ideas move faster when the right people show up to help.

KickStart 2025 was UBC BizTech's flagship startup event. A week where teams went from strangers to co-founders, and from pitch deck to paying customers. Judges asked when they could invest. Mentors stayed late to unblock progress. And students proved that when given a real opportunity, they ship.
