#### OVERVIEW

# Vancouver's largest student-run tech conference.

BluePrint 2026 brought 200+ students together at UBC Sauder School of Business downtown campus for a full-day experience to explore, connect, and "Find Their Place in Tech." Every detail, from curated coffee chats to hands-on workshops, was designed to make the tech industry feel more accessible and inclusive for students of all backgrounds.

As a Partnerships Director, I individually secured 7 partner booths, a keynote speech, a product demo from Internet Backyard, and $3k in sponsorships.

I also got us a live interview on Global News.
